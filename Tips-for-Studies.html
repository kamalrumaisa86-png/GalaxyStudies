1. Master Your Environment

• The "One Task" Zone: Clear your desk of everything except what you need for the specific subject you are working on. • Digital Silence: Put your phone in another room or use a "Focus Mode" to block notifications.

2. Use Active Recall

• The Blank Page Test: After reading a chapter or a summary, close the book and write down everything you remember. • Teach Someone Else: Explain a concept in simple terms to a family member or friend.

3. Smart Note Management

• Digital Backups: Keep your notes organized in a digital space like Google Keep with clear labels. • The 24-Hour Review: Briefly look over new notes within 24 hours to improve retention.

4. Time Management

• The Pomodoro Technique: Work for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. • Eat the Frog: Tackle the hardest or most boring task first.
